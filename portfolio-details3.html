Interpretable Machine Learning for Gender Specific Prediction of Mortality

This study unveils a gender-specific machine-learning model for predicting all-cause mortality. Women's mortality is linked to aging, while men are more affected by illness. Women tend to live ten years longer. The research highlights the significance of gender-specific variables for better mortality management and personalized urgent care.
